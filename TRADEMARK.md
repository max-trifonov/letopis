  affiliation that does not exist (e.g. a domain, app name, or social

- Using the Marks on merchandise, in a company/product name, or in

A fork or downstream distribution remains fully permitted under the
Apache License — it just needs its own name once it stops being an
unmodified copy.

## Requesting permission

Email **mp.trifonov@gmail.com** with what you'd like to do and where. We
default to yes for anything that doesn't create confusion about who
operates the official project.

## Why this exists

Letopis is open-core (ADR-012 in the project's design log): the engine
here is Apache 2.0 with no artificial limits, and a hosted version is
planned separately. This policy protects the name, not the code — anyone
can run, modify, and redistribute Letopis; they just can't call their own
service "Letopis" while doing it.
